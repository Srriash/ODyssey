Growth Curve Workbench

Your growth-curve odyssey, charted.

ODyssey turns messy plate reader files into a repeatable journey for bacterial growth curves - upload, map, fit, compare, and export in minutes. It can also support other time-series growth assays with similar kinetics. From first data point to final report, every step is designed to keep your analysis on course.
